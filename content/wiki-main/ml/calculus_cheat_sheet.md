## Gradient

A vector field in the direction of steepest ascent.

$$
\nabla f( x\_{1},  x\_{2}, \dots  x\_{i}) =  \begin{bmatrix}
  \frac{\partial f}{\partial x\_{1}} \\\\ 
  \frac{\partial f}{\partial x\_{2}} \\\\\
  \vdots \\\\\
  \frac{\partial f}{\partial x\_{i}}
\end{bmatrix} 
$$
